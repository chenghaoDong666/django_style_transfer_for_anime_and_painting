Wheat field and cypress

Wheat field and cypress is a landscape oil painting created by Van Gogh in July 1889. 73 cm x 93.5 cm. New York Metropolitan Museum of art.

Pond water lily

Pond water lily (original name of French work: Le bassinaux nympheas): specification 99 × 93 cm, created between 1918 and 1919. It is one of Monet's important late works with the theme of water lily.

Apples and oranges

Apples and oranges is an oil painting created by French painter Cezanne in 1899, with a size of 740 x 930mm. It is now collected in the Museum of Orsay, France.
